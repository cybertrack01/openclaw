## What We Look For

**Non-negotiables:**
- Genuine grooming experience (salon preferred over mobile-only)
- Portfolio of completed grooms

**Strong signals:**
- Salon environment experience (weighted higher than mobile or pet store)
- Warm, enthusiastic communication style
- Breed-specific knowledge and finish quality in portfolio
- Visa with 6+ months remaining (for WHV candidates)

**Red flags:**
- No portfolio after repeated requests
- Disengaged or minimal responses during screening
- Visa expiring within 2 months
